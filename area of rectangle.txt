Write a program to calculate area of rectangle-:

Ans
w = float(input('Please Enter the Width of a Rectangle: '))
h = float(input('Please Enter the Height of a Rectangle: '))
Area = w * h
print("\n Area of a Rectangle is: %.2f" %Area)
